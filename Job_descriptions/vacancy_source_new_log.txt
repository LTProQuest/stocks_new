<p>Babcock is a leading provider of critical, complex engineering services which support national defence, save lives and protect communities.</p>

<p>We focus on three highly regulated markets &ndash; defence, emergency services and civil nuclear &ndash; delivering vital services and managing complex assets in the UK and internationally.</p>

<p>We are a trusted partner who understands the key roles that our technology, our expertise, our infrastructure and our assets play in ensuring our customers can deliver.</p>

<p>So whether you want to be an engineer, a commercial manager or a safety specialist, we&rsquo;ve got a world-class graduate programme for you.</p>

<p>We have opportunities for graduates from nearly every degree subject. For most programmes we look for a 2:2 degree in a relevant subject. This will form the basis of your ongoing professional development. For engineers, this results in an Incorporated or Chartered status, and relevant qualifications for our Business Management programmes.</p>

<p>Babcock has over 268 sites in the UK. Where you&rsquo;re based depends on which team you join, but we ask that all our graduates are flexible and willing to move during their programme.
